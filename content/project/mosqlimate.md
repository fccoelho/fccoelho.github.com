## About

Mosqlimate is a data science platform designed to produce usable forecasts of dengue burden, incorporating all available data sources with special attention to the impact of future climate trends.

## Goals

- Develop forecasting models for dengue incidence
- Integrate climate and epidemiological data
- Ensure reproducibility of the entire forecasting pipeline
- Track data, model source code, and predictions

## Funding

This project is funded by the **Wellcome Trust**.
